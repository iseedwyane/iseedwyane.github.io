Stable High-Speed Quadrotor Flight using Differential Trajectory
Ying Wu, Yun Xie, Sen Li, and Depei Wang.
Mathematical Problems in Engineering, 2021

This study designed a rotation matrix-based adaptive controller to address the issue of quadrotor high-speed flight(velocity > 8m/s). By reducing the velocity when the attitude error is large, the quadrotor can achieve high-speed stable flight. The simulation results demonstrate that the control method can stabilize the quadrotor flight up to 10.5 m/s.

paper


Adaptive Control Method for High Speed Stable Trajectory of Quadrotor
Ying Wu, Yun Xie, Sen Li, and Li Li.
Modern Electronics Technique, 2021

This study optimized the adaptive controller based on differential trajectory to achieve rapid stabilization of the quadrotor when it encounters various disturbances. The difficulty of this study is the rapid attitude correction and the increased response speed. The control method can guarantee the convergence of the position error without reducing the control thrust when the attitude greatly deviates from the desired attitude. The simulations is conducted on the tethered quadrotor and verify that the controller can improve the robustness of the quadrotor against aerodynamic drag and mass change in high-speed flight.
