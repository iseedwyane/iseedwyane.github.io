CV

Sen Li received the BEng degree in Electronic Engineering from Guangdong University of Technology, Guangzhou, China, in 2019. He is pursuing his MEng degree in Control Science and Engineering at the Guangdong University of Technology, Guangdong, China. and as a third-year graduate student in Jet Power & Humanoid Robotics Lab. His research interests include biomimetic strategies, dynamics, control, and algorithmic applications.

Zhifeng HUANG, Sen LI, Jungao JIANG, and Ying WU
IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters (RA-L), 2021
